How to Create a Prolific Account

Follow these steps to sign up for a Prolific account as a research participant.

Step 1: Visit the Prolific Website

Go to Prolific's official website.

Step 2: Click on "Sign Up"

On the homepage, click the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner.

Step 3: Choose "Participant" Account

When prompted, select "I want to take part in studies" to proceed.

Step 4: Enter Your Details

Fill in your email, create a password, and enter your basic personal information.

Step 5: Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link inside to confirm your account.

Step 6: Complete Your Profile

Log in to your account and complete your demographic profile to qualify for studies.

Step 7: Start Taking Studies

Once your profile is complete, browse available studies and start participating.
