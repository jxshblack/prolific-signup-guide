How to Create a Prolific Account

Follow these steps to sign up for a Prolific account as a research participant.

Step 1:

Visit Prolific's website here --> Prolific's official website.

Step 2:

Click on "Sign In" in the top right corner if you have an account. Click "Start Earning" if you are new.

Step 3:

Fill in your email, create a password, and enter your basic personal information.

Step 4:

Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link inside to confirm your account.

Step 5:

Log in to your account and complete your demographic profile to qualify for studies.
