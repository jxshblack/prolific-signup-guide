How to Create a Prolific Account

Follow these steps to sign up for a Prolific account as a research participant.

Step 1:

Visit Prolific's website here --> Prolific's official website.

Step 2:

In the top right corner, if you have an account then please select "Sign In". If not, please select "Start Earning" and continue following this guide.

Step 3:

Fill in your email, create a password, and enter your basic personal information. Once you have completed this form, please select "Next".

Step 4:

Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link inside to confirm your account. Once verified, you will be added to Prolific's participant waitlist.

Step 5:

Congratulations, you're in! If possible, log in to your account and complete your demographic profile to qualify for studies.
